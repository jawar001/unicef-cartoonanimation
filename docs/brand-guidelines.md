## 1. Brand Story

Château Barkalot is a dog café nestled in the spirit of Camelot — a place where coffee flows like the finest mead, pastries arrive like gifts from the royal kitchen, and every four-legged visitor earns a place at the round table. We exist for the pack: the pups, their people, and the whole warm, waggy community that gathers here.

We are not a corporate chain. We are a neighborhood hearth.

---

## 2. Voice & Tone

### Voice Archetype: The Jester-Sage

Speaks like a kindly royal herald who has clearly spent the morning giving belly rubs. Equal parts wit and warmth. Never pompous. Never saccharine.

### Tone Dimensions

| Dimension | Score (1–5) | Meaning |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Formal ↔ Casual | 2 | Casual with courtly flourishes |
| Serious ↔ Playful | 5 | Fully playful |
| Reserved ↔ Expressive | 4 | Expressive |
| Modern ↔ Timeless | 4 | Timeless wink |

### Voice Chart

| Trait | We Are | We Are Not |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Whimsical | Courtly, warm, story-driven | Ren-faire cosplay or cringe puns |
| Warm | Inviting, second-person, neighborly | Corporate, distant, "guests" |
| Playful | One earned pun per section | Stacked puns, forced wordplay |
| Timeless | Medieval flourishes that feel natural | Heavy Elizabethan — no "thee/thou" |

### Voice Rules

1. **Medieval vocabulary — sparingly.** Max **one** medieval phrase per paragraph: "hark," "verily," "pull up a tankard," "the round table awaits." Never go full Ren-faire.
2. **Dog puns are earned, not stacked.** Max **one** pun per section.
3. **Second person always.** "You" and "your pup" — never "customers," "guests," or "patrons."
4. **Active voice. Short sentences. Warmth over cleverness.**

## 3. Messaging Framework

### Tagline (≤7 words)
> **"Where every pup is knighted."**

### Value Proposition (≤20 words)
> A cozy Camelot for coffee, pastries, and the noblest pack of dogs in the realm.

### Mission
> We gather the pack — coffee in hand, pups at foot — so every soul in the realm feels at home.

### Key Phrases (use across all copy)

| Phrase | Best Used In |
|--------|-------------|
| "The round table awaits." | Hero CTA, reservation nudge |
| "Hark — coffee is served." | Menu headers, social posts |
| "Every pup, a knight." | About section, adoptable intro |
| "Pull up a tankard." | Menu CTA, events |
| "Gather the pack." | Events, community sections |

### Words to Use
`hark` · `noble` · `quest` · `round table` · `tankard` · `realm` · `pack` · `pup` · `paw-worthy` · `wag` · `cozy` · `gather`

### Words to Avoid
`leverage` · `synergy` · `solutions` · `premium` · `luxury` · `world-class` · `fur babies` (too saccharine) · `doggos` (brief-internal only)

## 6. AI Image Generation

### Base Style Prompt (prepend to all image prompts)
```
Pixar 3D cartoon style, soft subsurface scattering, painterly golden-hour lighting from camera-left, 
shallow depth of field, warm coral/purple/honey palette (#E85D4A #4A2C6B #D4A853), 
cozy Arthurian atmosphere, no text in image, no humans with visible logos, 
expressive oversized eyes on dog characters, stylized fur shader
```

### Lighting Consistency (knight cards)
Same golden-hour key light from camera-left at ~30°, soft warm fill from camera-right, subtle rim light on fur edges. Background blur consistent across all 18.

### Visual Don'ts

| Avoid | Reason |
|-------|--------|
| Flat corporate vector art | Brand is warm and storybook-illustrative |
| High-contrast noir lighting | This is Pixar, not grimdark |
| Text in images | SEO/accessibility — use alt text instead |
| Cold blue tones | Conflicts with warm golden palette |
| Pure black | Use espresso brown (#3D2314) as ink |
